Our Journey in Filmmaking

Since July 26, 2015, Divine Films & Entertainment has been transforming imaginative concepts into powerful visual stories that resonate worldwide.

Founded by visionaries Jyoti Pandey, Atul Pandey, and Arup Sur, we've grown from a passionate startup into a leading production house.

Today, we deliver award-winning films, digital series, and innovative content across multiple platforms — driven by storytelling excellence.
